Your Guide to Better Health

Practical tips for maintaining a healthy lifestyle in Sierra Leone

Stay Healthy with These Tips

Stay Hydrated

Drink at least 8 glasses of clean water daily to keep your body and brain functioning well. In Sierra Leone's tropical climate, proper hydration is especially important.

Learn more →

Eat Balanced Meals

Include locally available vegetables, fruits, proteins, and whole grains in your daily diet to boost immunity and maintain energy levels.

Learn more →

Exercise Regularly

Engage in at least 30 minutes of physical activity every day. Walking, dancing, or playing sports are great ways to stay active in our communities.

Learn more →

Get Enough Sleep

Aim for 7–8 hours of quality sleep every night to help your body recover, improve mental health, and maintain optimal daily functioning.

Learn more →

Wash Your Hands

Practice frequent handwashing with soap and clean water, especially before eating, after using the toilet, and when returning home.

Learn more →

Preventive Healthcare

Schedule regular checkups, vaccinations, and screenings for early detection of health issues. Many conditions are more treatable when caught early.
